here i will tell you about my recipes.. there are the dun-dun noodles, which is stole from my ecuadorian mistress. my favorite food is eggs, but only the kind that i made in seventh grade. seventh grade rachel doesn't exist anymore (all the bright and precious things fade so fast, and they don't come back) so unfortunatly my favorite food has been discontinued. however, i think someday if i have a daughter, i will be able to eat the seventh grader eggs again.

another excellent food is peppermint coffee. this is coffee, usuallly mostly milk, with a scoop of peppermint ice cream in it. eventually, the ice cream will melt and it will turn into a coffee creamer type things where at the end you are just drinking peppermint icecream with a tiny bit of coffee and milk in it. it's like a milkshake. another way to achieve this is by brushing your teeth and then drinking cold coffee immidietly afterwards.
